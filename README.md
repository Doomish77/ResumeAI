## 📋 What is ResumeAI?

ResumeAI is a web tool designed to help job seekers create professional resumes. It uses artificial intelligence to suggest content and improve your resume. You do not need any technical skills. The app offers easy editing, safe login, and good-looking resume templates. It works well with popular job application systems.

## 🔍 Key Features

- AI suggests personalized resume text  
- Live editing with instant preview  
- Secure user login to save your work  
- Multiple editable templates to choose from  
- Export resumes as PDF or Word files  
- Built to work with job systems that scan resumes  
- Simple dashboard to manage your profiles  

## 🖥️ System Requirements

To use ResumeAI on Windows, your PC should meet these minimum specs:

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- The app does not start: Make sure your Windows is up to date and you have enough disk space.  
- Cannot log in: Check your internet connection and password. You can reset your password on the login screen.  
- AI suggestions missing or slow: Confirm your internet is working. The AI needs to connect to the server.  
- Export fails: Try restarting the app or your PC. Also, check for enough free space.  

If problems continue, you can check the app’s Help section or community forum on the GitHub page.
